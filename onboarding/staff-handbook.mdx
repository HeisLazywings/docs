This handbook exists to give every member of the 5G Silver Bakery team a clear and consistent set of expectations. Whether you are new to the team or returning for another season, the policies here apply to you at all times while you are on shift. Reading and following this handbook is not optional — it is part of your responsibility as a member of staff. If you ever have a question about anything in this document, speak to your supervisor.

## Dress Code

Your appearance on shift reflects the standards of 5G Silver Bakery and the wider 5G Silver Square complex. Customers and colleagues expect a professional, hygienic environment, and your uniform is a key part of that.

- Clean staff uniform must be worn at all times during your shift. Arrive in your uniform or change into it before your shift begins — do not start work in personal clothing.
- Hair must be tied back and fully covered with a hair net whenever you are in the kitchen or any food preparation area, no exceptions.
- No jewellery — including rings, bracelets, and earrings — is permitted in food preparation areas. This applies even if the jewellery is small or seems unlikely to cause harm.
- Non-slip, closed-toe shoes are required on the kitchen floor at all times. Open-toe sandals, heels, or smooth-soled footwear are not permitted.

## Attendance & Punctuality

Reliability is one of the most important qualities we look for in every member of staff. The bakery runs on tight schedules, and a missing team member puts pressure on everyone else.

- Report to work **10 minutes before your shift starts** so you are ready to begin at the scheduled time, not still preparing.
- If you are unable to attend your shift for any reason, you must notify your supervisor at least **2 hours before** your shift is due to start. Contact the bakery by phone or WhatsApp on **+234 905 500 0737**.
- Three unexcused absences within any rolling period may result in formal disciplinary action. An absence is only excused if you have informed your supervisor in advance and the reason has been acknowledged.

<Warning>
  Repeated lateness or unexcused absence will be addressed formally by management. Consistent reliability is a condition of your continued employment at 5G Silver Bakery.
</Warning>

## Conduct & Professionalism

Every person who visits 5G Silver Bakery should leave with a positive experience. How you present yourself and treat others — customers and colleagues alike — is central to that.

- Always greet customers warmly and politely. A simple, friendly welcome makes a lasting impression and sets the tone for their visit.
- Mobile phones must be kept on silent and out of sight during working hours. Personal calls and messaging are not permitted while you are on the floor or in the kitchen. Use your break time for personal phone use.
- Respect all colleagues regardless of their role or seniority. A respectful, supportive team environment makes everyone's work easier and more enjoyable.
- Report any disputes, concerns, or incidents involving colleagues or customers to your supervisor immediately. Do not attempt to resolve serious conflicts on your own.

## Health & Safety

5G Silver Bakery takes the health and safety of its staff and customers seriously. You have a personal responsibility to follow these rules every single shift.

- Report any injury to a supervisor immediately, no matter how minor it may seem. Small cuts and burns can become serious if untreated, and all incidents must be logged.
- Do not handle food if you are unwell with vomiting, diarrhoea, a skin infection, or any other condition that could contaminate food. Inform your supervisor before your shift begins so cover can be arranged.
- Know the location of the **fire extinguisher** and **first aid kit** in your work area. If you are unsure where these are, ask your supervisor on your first shift.

<Note>
  Food safety is everyone's responsibility. When in doubt, throw it out. Never serve or use any product you are unsure about — the cost of wasted food is always less than the cost of a customer falling ill.
</Note>

## Pay & Benefits

Staff at 5G Silver Bakery are paid on the **28th of each month**. Payment will be made via the agreed method at the time of your employment. If the 28th falls on a weekend or public holiday, confirm the payment date with your manager in advance.

Any queries about your pay — including discrepancies, deductions, or changes to your hours — should be directed to the manager promptly and in private. Do not raise pay disputes on the floor during a shift.
