Welcome to the **5G Silver Bakery** family! We are so glad to have you on board. Located **Opposite Silver Jubilee Quarters, Sokoto Road, Samaru, Zaria**, our bakery and restaurant is a proud part of the wider **5G Silver Square** complex — a vibrant, multi-service destination that also includes a Gym, Game House, VIP Lounge, Barbershop, Suya Spot, Kids Playground, and a 5-a-Side Football Pitch. You are joining a team that is passionate about great food, genuine hospitality, and making every customer feel at home. This documentation will help you settle in, understand our standards, and do your best work every single day.

---

## Our Mission

At 5G Silver Bakery, our mission is simple: to provide the people of Zaria with **fresh, high-quality baked goods and meals** — every day, without exception. From the first loaf out of the oven in the morning to the last order served at close of business, we hold ourselves to the highest standard of taste, consistency, and care. We believe that food is more than sustenance — it is a source of joy, comfort, and community. Everything we do, we do with **pride and professionalism**.
