Following the opening and closing procedures consistently is essential to running a safe, clean, and well-organised bakery. These checklists must be completed every single shift — no exceptions.

## Morning Opening Checklist

Complete all tasks between **7:15 AM and 7:30 AM** before the doors open.

- [ ] Arrive at least 15 minutes before opening time.
- [ ] Collect keys from supervisor and unlock premises.
- [ ] Switch on lights, ventilation fans, and oven for preheating.
- [ ] Check that all refrigeration units are at correct temperature (below 5°C).
- [ ] Review the day's recipe and order schedule with the supervisor.
- [ ] Wash hands thoroughly and put on uniform, hair net, and apron.
- [ ] Prepare workstations: clean surfaces, lay out tools and baking trays.
- [ ] Set out display cases and countertop items (labels, tongs, bags).
- [ ] Check stock levels and flag any shortages to the supervisor.
- [ ] Confirm the till is ready and change is available.
- [ ] Unlock the front door and place the OPEN sign at exactly 7:30 AM.

<Note>
  Do not open the front door before 7:30 AM even if customers are waiting outside.
</Note>

## Evening Closing Checklist

Begin closing tasks **30 minutes before close** — 8:30 PM on weekdays, 9:30 PM on weekends.

- [ ] Announce last orders 30 minutes before closing time.
- [ ] Wrap and label any remaining baked goods for storage or disposal.
- [ ] Discard any items that cannot be safely stored overnight.
- [ ] Switch off oven, grill, and other cooking equipment. Confirm all are off.
- [ ] Clean oven interior and baking trays.
- [ ] Wipe down all counters, prep surfaces, and the display case.
- [ ] Mop kitchen and customer-area floors.
- [ ] Empty all bins and replace bin liners.
- [ ] Wash and put away all utensils, boards, and bowls.
- [ ] Return all perishables to refrigeration. Ensure all fridges are closed properly.
- [ ] Count and reconcile the till. Record the closing total in the log book.
- [ ] Switch off all lights, ventilation, and non-essential equipment.
- [ ] Lock all doors and windows. Set the alarm if applicable.
- [ ] Hand keys to the supervisor and confirm handover.

<Warning>
  Never leave the premises without confirming the oven and all cooking equipment are completely off.
</Warning>

## Weekly Deep Clean Tasks

Carry out these additional tasks every **Friday evening** after the regular closing checklist is complete. Record completion in the cleaning log.

- Clean behind and underneath all equipment (ovens, fridges, prep tables).
- Descale the kettle and any steam equipment.
- Wipe down walls, shelving units, and storage containers.
- Wash all reusable bags, aprons, and cloths.
- Check expiry dates on all dry stock and condiments.
- Record completion of deep clean in the cleaning log.
