Our drinks menu complements the food menu and is a key part of the customer experience. Each drink should be prepared fresh and served at the correct temperature.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Zobo">
    **Yield:** ~2 litres | **Prep:** 20 min | **Chill:** 2 hours minimum

    **Ingredients**

    - [ ] 100 g dried hibiscus petals (zobo leaves)
    - [ ] 2 litres water
    - [ ] 1 medium pineapple (peeled and chopped, or 200 ml pineapple juice)
    - [ ] 2 tbsp fresh ginger (grated)
    - [ ] 4 cloves
    - [ ] 1 tsp citric acid or juice of 1 lemon
    - [ ] Sugar to taste

    **Method**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Boil and infuse">
        Boil water. Add hibiscus, ginger, cloves, and pineapple. Boil for 15 minutes.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add acid and steep">
        Remove from heat. Add citric acid or lemon juice. Steep for 10 minutes.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Strain and chill">
        Strain through a fine sieve. Sweeten to taste. Cool and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Serve">
        Serve chilled over ice in a tall glass.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Note>Zobo is best prepared the evening before for maximum flavour.</Note>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Banana Smoothie">
    **Yield:** 2 glasses | **Prep:** 5 min

    **Ingredients**

    - [ ] 2 ripe bananas (frozen if possible)
    - [ ] 300 ml cold whole milk (or plant-based milk)
    - [ ] 2 tbsp honey
    - [ ] 1 tsp vanilla extract
    - [ ] 4 ice cubes

    **Method**

    - Add all ingredients to blender.
    - Blend on high for 60 seconds until smooth.
    - Taste and adjust sweetness.
    - Pour into chilled glasses and serve immediately.

    <Tip>Use frozen bananas instead of ice for a thicker, creamier texture.</Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Mocktail">
    We rotate mocktail flavours based on availability. Below is our standard house recipe.

    **House Mocktail: Sunset Twist** | **Yield:** 1 glass | **Prep:** 3 min

    **Ingredients**

    - [ ] 60 ml orange juice (freshly squeezed)
    - [ ] 30 ml grenadine syrup
    - [ ] 30 ml lime juice
    - [ ] 150 ml chilled sparkling water or Sprite
    - [ ] Ice cubes
    - [ ] Orange slice and mint sprig (to garnish)

    **Method**

    - Fill glass with ice.
    - Add orange juice and lime juice.
    - Pour grenadine over the back of a spoon to create a layered effect.
    - Top up with sparkling water.
    - Garnish with orange slice and mint. Serve immediately.

    <Note>Do not stir after adding sparkling water — the layered effect is part of the presentation.</Note>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Lebanese Tea">
    **Yield:** 4 cups | **Prep:** 10 min

    **Ingredients**

    - [ ] 4 cups water
    - [ ] 2 tsp loose black tea or 2 tea bags
    - [ ] 4 cardamom pods (lightly crushed)
    - [ ] 2 cinnamon sticks
    - [ ] Sugar to taste
    - [ ] Fresh mint leaves (to serve)

    **Method**

    - Bring water to a boil. Add cardamom and cinnamon; simmer for 5 minutes.
    - Add tea; steep for 3 minutes.
    - Strain into a teapot or cups. Add sugar to taste.
    - Garnish each cup with fresh mint leaves.

    <Tip>Lebanese tea is typically served very sweet. Let customers specify their preference.</Tip>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
