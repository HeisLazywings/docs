## Quality Standards

All baked goods at 5G Silver Bakery are held to a strict freshness standard. You must observe the following rules at all times:

- All baked goods must be made fresh daily. Do not serve day-old stock.
- Label and date all prepared items stored in the fridge.
- Discard any item that smells off, looks discoloured, or has been stored longer than 24 hours.

<Tip>
  When in doubt about freshness, ask your supervisor before serving.
</Tip>
